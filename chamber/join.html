NP membership Benefits

Our Non-Profit membership is designed specifically for non-profit organizations in the masaka region.

  • Free membership with no annual fees
  • Listing in our non-profit business directory
  • Access to networking events
  • monthly newsletter with funding opportunities
  • Participation in community outreach programs
  • Quarterly non-profit roundtable discussions
×

Bronze membership Benefits

Our Bronze membership provides essential benefits for small businesses and startups.

  • Annual fee: $150
  • Business listing in our online directory
  • Access to monthly networking events
  • Subscription to our business newsletter
  • 10% discount on chamber-sponsored workshops
  • Basic social media promotion (1 post per quarter)
×

Silver membership Benefits

Our Silver membership offers enhanced benefits for growing businesses.

  • Annual fee: $300
  • All Bronze benefits plus:
  • Featured listing in our online directory
  • Access to exclusive business seminars
  • 20% discount on chamber-sponsored workshops
  • Priority registration for events
  • monthly social media promotion (1 post per month)
  • Quarterly business spotlight opportunity
×

Gold membership Benefits

Our Gold membership provides premium benefits for established businesses.

  • Annual fee: $500
  • All Silver benefits plus:
  • Premium featured listing in our directory
  • VIP access to all chamber events
  • Free admission to chamber workshops
  • Weekly social media promotion
  • monthly business spotlight feature
  • Annual recognition at chamber gala
  • Dedicated business consultant sessions
  • Opportunity to sponsor chamber events
