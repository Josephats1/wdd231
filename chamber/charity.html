Site Name

Jerry Fly Charity Org
This name was selected because it reflects the spirit of the organization’s founder and its mission to help people fly above poverty, hopelessness, and limitation through charity, creativity, and empowerment.
Optional domain availability: jerryflycharity.org

Site Purpose

The site will provide information about Jerry Fly Charity Organization’s programs, ways to donate or volunteer, real-life stories of impact, and a platform for creative youth to connect, share, and grow. It will also include upcoming events, news, and how to get involved in community development and talent-building initiatives.

Scenarios

  • How can I donate to support the creative youth programs of Jerry Fly Charity Org?
  • What opportunities are available for young people to join and benefit from the organization?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #2b6777 – Used for headings, navigation links, and footer background.
  • Accent Color: #f2be22 – Used for navigation background and highlights.
  • Background Color: #f9f9f9 – Applied to the overall page background for a clean look.

Typography

  • Headings: Times New Roman, serif – For a classic and formal tone.
  • Body Text: Segoe UI, Tahoma, sans-serif – For readability and modern feel.
