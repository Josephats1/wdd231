Our Journey

From humble beginnings to global impact, our story is one of hope, perseverance, and transformation.

2008

Foundation Established

Founded by Dr. Sarah Johnson and a group of passionate volunteers with a mission to bring hope to underserved communities.

2010

First School Built

Completed our first school in Kenya, providing education to 200 children in a rural community.

2013

Healthcare Initiative

Launched mobile health clinics to serve remote areas in Guatemala and Honduras.

2016

Global Expansion

Expanded operations to Asia with clean water projects in Bangladesh and education programs in India.

2020

Pandemic Response

Mobilized emergency relief efforts to provide food, medicine, and education support during COVID-19.

2023

150,000 Lives Impacted

Reached the milestone of serving over 150,000 beneficiaries across 45 countries worldwide.
